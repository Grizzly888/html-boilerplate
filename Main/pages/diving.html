Freediving

Freediving is the practice of holding your breath when diving underwater without the use of breathing equipment, such as a scuba tank.

Freediving, despite being classified as an extreme sport is actually a very relaxing, calm sport that can be enjoyed by people of all ages and abilities.
The sport encourages participants to progress at their own speed with focus on the experience of freediving rather than any goal or target to reach.
